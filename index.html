🧁 Our Story

Kampala's Favourite Bakery Destination

Yummy – A Tasteful Moment is a beloved bakery and dessert café in Kampala, with locations at Palm Village Mall, Ggaba, and our original home at Lugogo Mall, Kampala, where our journey first began.
We bake artisan breads daily, craft custom cakes for every celebration, and serve creamy gelato in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. From our signature sourdough loaves, slow-fermented overnight, to our indulgent Red Velvet and Tiramisu cakes created by our skilled pastry chefs — every item is made with love and the finest ingredients. Whether you're grabbing a morning coffee and croissant, celebrating a birthday with a custom cake, or treating yourself to a scoop of gelato, Yummy is your tasteful moment.
